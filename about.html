About Us

The ideal cup of coffee requires many hands, including those of the farmers who tend to the coffee cherries, the expert roasters who extract the greatest flavor from each bean, and the barista who carefully serves it.In addition to embracing our legacy and innovating to provide fresh, satisfying experiences, we are dedicated to the highest standards of quality and service.

Along the cobblestone walkways of Seattle's famed Pike Place Market, our tale starts in 1971. It was at this location that Starbucks launched its first shop, providing our customers with freshly roasted coffee beans, tea, and spices from all over the world to take home. The famous story "Moby-Dick," which recalls the maritime heritage of the early coffee dealers, served as the basis for our name.
